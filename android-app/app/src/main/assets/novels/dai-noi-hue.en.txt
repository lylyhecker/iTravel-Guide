The moon hung over Hue like a silver lantern, washing the mossy roofs of the Imperial City in light. Le Chi, a young palace attendant, slipped through the endless corridors to deliver a secret letter to the prince confined inside the Forbidden Purple City. Every step echoed like the whispers of the dynasty.

The crimson walls seemed to keep the breath of emperors past. For a heartbeat, Le Chi swore she heard the zither of a forgotten princess, mingling with sandalwood drifting from the ancestral temple. She wondered if history might change should the letter reach its destined hands.

A guard's shadow stretched across the path. Le Chi had to choose between melting back into darkness or stepping boldly into the light. Clutching the letter, heart pounding, she moved toward the gate that would decide her fate.
